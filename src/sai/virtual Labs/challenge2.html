1. How to write 60 is not greater than 70?





Solution:

60 is not greater than 70 is mathematically represented as 60 < 70.

2. What are the other terms used to describe less than?





Solution:

The other terms used to describe less than is smaller than, lower than, below, fewer than, not greater than, and so on.

3. Fill in the blanks with the suitable sign: 90 - 10 ____ 100





Solution:

here, 90 - 10 => 80, therefore 80 < 100

4. Nirmal got 40 marks in her Maths exam whereas her friend Ritu got 24 marks in the same exam. Who scored fewer marks?





Solution:

Marks obtained by Nirmal = 40

Marks obtained by Ritu = 24

Since, 24 is less than 40, 24<40

Therefore, Ritu scored less than Nirmal.
