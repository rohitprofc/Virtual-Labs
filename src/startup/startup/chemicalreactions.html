What's a Chemical Reaction?

A chemical reaction is like a magical change where different things mix and create something new. Just like when you mix colors to make a new color, chemicals mix to make new stuff too!

For example, when you mix baking soda and vinegar, they have a chemical reaction. They make carbon dioxide gas, which creates bubbles and fizz!
