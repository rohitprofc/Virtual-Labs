Circle

A circle is a round-shaped figure that has no corners or edges.

In geometry, a circle can be defined as a closed shape, two-dimensional shape, curved shape.

Square

A square is a four-sided polygon with equal sides and equal angles.

In geometry, a square is a two-dimensional shape with all sides of equal length.

Rectangle

A rectangle is a four-sided polygon with opposite sides of equal length and all angles of 90 degrees.

In geometry, a rectangle is a two-dimensional shape with two pairs of parallel sides.

A triangle is a three-sided polygon with three angles.

In geometry, triangles can have various types, such as equilateral, isosceles, and scalene.
