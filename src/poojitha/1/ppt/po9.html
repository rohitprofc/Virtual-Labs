Consumer behavior with old electronics worldwide 2021

Published by
Bruna Alves,
Apr 17, 2023
A survey of more than 13,000 people across multiple countries found that 31 percent of consumers throw away old electronics in their regular household garbage or mix it them with other recyclable materials. A quarter of respondents said they try to repair their older products, while 44 percent of consumers said they find a specialty place to recycle their devices.

The information does not usually directly identify you, but it can give you a more personalized web experience. Because we respect your right to privacy, you can choose not to allow some types of cookies. Click on the different category headings to find out more and change our default settings.
